Verbs in Tonolog

  • Eγā́y – I
  • Juā́y – You (singular)
  • Onerā́y – He
  • Onā́y – She
  • Etā́y – It
  • Eγā́jet – We
  • Juā́jet – You (plural)
  • Onerā́jet – They (Male)
  • Onā́jet – They (Female)
  • Etā́jet – They (Mixed/Undecisive/Neuter)
  • Huèt – Who (singular or plural)

  • Eγā́je – I am
  • Juā́jes – You are (singular)
  • Onerā́jek – He is
  • Onā́jek – She is
  • Etā́jek, Èt’ek – It is, It’s
  • Eγā́jeme – We are
  • Juā́jetese – You are (plural)
  • Onerā́jete – They are (Male)
  • Onā́jeteke – They are (Female)
  • Etā́jeteke – They are (Neuter)
  • Huètek – Who is
